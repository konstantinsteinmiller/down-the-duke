Gameplay
Level Completion Time: 2.5-3 minutes
Total Phases: Four Phases
Phase Completion Time: 30-45 seconds

-

(If Time Allows) Pre Level Cutscene - Look up at the insane island fortress, lightning flashes.

Level Start - You are at the bottom of the Duke’s island fortress: it’s a power plant. Waves crash on the rocks as the
camera pushes in. We hear two metal clamping sounds. Clink! Clunk!
The Player begins to be pulled upward. Everything is quiet except the sound of the incoming storm and the crashing
waves.

Phase 1 introduces:
Aiming
Standard shots
Charged shots
Containment Encounters

Phase 1 - Tutorial (Total Time to Complete: 25 sec)
A red light on a power container flashes. It rests nestled on the side of the rock. On the container is a yellow hazard
sign. Blow it up to begin the game.
A “Power” Meter UI appears on the left and drains upward. A quarter bar is lost.
The level begins to scroll.
Large pipes lead upward and branch into two separate containers.
Ctnr 1 and Ctnr 2, both have a flashing red light with a hazard sign.
If you miss these, you won’t deplete the Duke’s health – disguised as the “Power” Meter.
The level rises past the small containers and onto a new rocky floor. Ka-chunk. It stops.
Harmless Encounter: Big Containment (HP: 4) (Total Time to Complete: 10 - 15 sec)
Large industrial containment center
Door cycles open/shut (1.8 seconds open/1 second shut)
Minecarts exit during open state (only for worldbuilding)
Red flashing weak point is visible internally
Alarm state triggers after damage threshold
Weakpoint vulnerable only during open state
After 2 damage:
Trigger animated alarms
Deploy Iron Gate over door/weak point
Iron Gate HP:
4 standard shots
OR 2 charged shots
On-screen text flashes: CHARGE UP!
After gate destruction:
weak point re-exposed
Final 2 damage destroys containment
On destruction:
Power Meter drains up a quarter bar.
Ascent resumes into Phase 2.

Enter Phase 2 (there is no stopping between Phases, only documentation)
Phase 2 introduces:
The Catch-Can mechanic
Timing Enemy Fire
Mid-air Parrying and Deflecting
Red Cannonballs

Gameplay
An enemy cannon flashes. A black cannonball is fired directly at the Player. You hear it whistling as it careens toward
you. THUNK. The shot trajectory was intentionally centered and you caught it! Welcome to the Catch-Can mechanic! The
cannonball is glowing red-hot. Fire back for double damage!
BIG explosion. Mm, satisfying.
A second cannon ball is shot, but it isn’t intentionally centered. The Player can parry.
If the Player is hit, the Player’s health bar appears.
The Player is down 1 health.
Player can fire back. These cannons are 1 HP.
(camera still scrolling) Another cannon fires. The Player can parry.
If not, the Player is down 2 health.
Player can fire back.
After a moment, a cannon fires a red cannonball. It’s slow and wobbles fiercely.
On-screen text flashes (twice): PARRY.
4 seconds until impact.
Return the red ball back into the cannon: cannon explodes.
Another cannon fires a red ball
On-screen text flashes (twice): PARRY – for the last time.
Return the red ball back into the cannon: cannon explodes.
A black cannonball is fired. The Player can parry.
If not, the Player is down 3 health.
Player can fire back.
You pass by small containers along the rock wall. Shoot them all to further deplete the Power Meter. Miss them and save
some more health for the Duke (player doesn’t know this; possible replay value)
If the Player shoots them all, they are rewarded full health back.
The level rises into Phase 3.

Enter Phase 3
Phase 3 introduces:
A mechanical recap of Phases 1 and 2, plus cannon cooldowns:
Quick Aim
Punishing Cooldowns
Armed Encounters (bigger than before)

Gameplay
Armed Encounter: Power Station (HP: 6)
Two cannons (Left/Right)
Cannons alternate firing (not simultaneous by default)
Player actions:
Parry black and red cannonballs, or catch-can a black ball.
State 2 – Cooldown Window
Triggered after a short firing sequence:
Cannons enter cooldown: 7 seconds (overheat state)
Cannons stop firing briefly
Iron Gate becomes vulnerable!
Damage the Iron Gate during this window
State 3 – Destroy the Exposed Core
Gate breaks open
The core is fully exposed! Blast it!
Cannons resume firing in a staggered pattern
Optional: Cannon Destruction
Cannons can be destroyed independently during cooldown windows
On destruction:
Power Meter drains upward
Ascent resumes.
The Cannon Corridor
As the player ascends, they enter a corridor lined with cannons.
Survive the fire and blast nearby power containers.
Armed Encounter: Four Corner Power Containers (each HP: 4),
Four corner cannons fire sequentially in a rotating loop.
Only one fires at a time
Order: Top Left, Top Right, Bottom Right, Bottom Left.
Red Cannonballs Only (2 damage)
Speed Increase for second rotation
Clear them all to advance into Phase 4.

Enter Phase 4
Phase 4 introduces:
Wind resistance mechanics
Fun, off-set mechanic

## Abilities:

**Charged Shot Intercept**
If a charged shot collides with an oncoming cannonball:
First cannonball explodes
Second cannonball continues into enemy ship
Damage is reduced slightly:
1.25× stronger than a standard black shot

**Catch-Can Mechanic**
If an enemy cannonball somehow flies into the player’s cannon barrel:
Projectile is caught
Can immediately be fired back for Double Damage and double the speed
this is indicated by a red cannonball even if the player shoots a normal tap shot
A text overlay flashes: "Catch-Can: Fire back for DOUBLE DAMAGE!" and fades out softly. Make the text break nicely.
